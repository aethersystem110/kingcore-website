Who We Are

Built on
strength & precision

King Core, operating under Imperial Synergies, is a leading paper tube winding manufacturer based in Lahore, Pakistan. We transform raw materials — strawboard, coreboard, and finishing layers — into precision-engineered paper cores that serve as the structural foundation for industries worldwide.

Our facility operates with meticulous quality control, ensuring every core meets exact specifications for diameter, wall thickness, and crush strength. From lightweight textile cones to heavy-duty industrial tubes, we deliver consistency at scale.
