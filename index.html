Chapter One

The Raw

Where every core begins

Strawboard The bulk. Recycled straw fibers compressed into rigid sheets. This is the body of the core — where strength begins. Measured in kilograms
Coreboard The spine. Dense, high-GSM board that determines crush resistance. One layer can bear hundreds of kilograms. Measured in kilograms
Finishing Layer The face. A smooth outer wrap for printability and surface quality. What the world sees. Measured in kilograms
Dextrine The bond. A natural starch adhesive that fuses every layer into one. Invisible, but it holds everything together. Measured in bags
Chapter Two

The Craft

01

Selection

Raw materials arrive. Each batch is tested for moisture, weight, and fiber integrity. Only what passes moves forward.

02

Mixing

Dextrine adhesive is prepared to exact viscosity. Too thin and the layers slip. Too thick and they crack. Precision matters.

03

Winding

Sheets spiral around a steel mandrel under controlled tension. Layer after layer, the tube takes shape. This is where the core is born.

04

Cutting

Continuous tubes are sliced to exact lengths. Millimeter precision. Each cut verified against order specifications.

05

Inspection

Crush test. Dimension check. Surface scan. Every core is judged. The ones that pass carry our name. The rest don't leave.

06

Dispatch

Bundled, labeled, loaded. Our logistics ensure your lines never stop. The core leaves our floor and enters your world.
