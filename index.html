01

Raw Strips

Sheets of strawboard, coreboard, and finishing layers — the raw ingredients of every core.

02

Spiral Winding

Strips feed onto a steel mandrel, spiraling layer over layer under controlled tension. The tube takes shape.

03

Grinding

A precision grinder shaves the surface. Dust flies. What remains is dimensionally perfect.

04

Waxing

A hot wax coat seals the surface — smooth, protective, and ready for high-speed applications.

05

Cutting

The continuous tube meets the blade. Precise lengths, every time.

06

Chamfering

Rough edges are beveled smooth. The final touch before a core earns the King Core name.
