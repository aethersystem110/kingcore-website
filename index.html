What we do

Every roll of film, every bolt of fabric, every reel of tape — in Pakistan and across our export markets — rests on a paper core. Ours are made to be invisible — because when they work, no one thinks about them.

01 / 03
01 · Construction

Spiral-wound with zero compromise.

Multi-ply kraft paper, fused under tension, cut to length with precision tolerance. Every core in every batch is dimensionally identical — because when it arrives on your production floor, it has to drop straight into place.

02 · Specifications

Built to your exact requirements.

Inner diameters from 3-inch through 6-inch as standard, with custom sizing on request. Wall thickness engineered to your load capacity. Lengths cut to your equipment. Your spec, our job.

03 · Scale

From prototype runs to production volume.

Whether you need fifty cores or fifty thousand, we run the same quality control across every order. No minimum orders that don't make sense, no maximum that constrains your production schedule.
